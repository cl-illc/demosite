[UTTER](https://he-utter.eu) (October 2022 -- September 2025) was a Horizon Europe collaboration amongst UvA (coordinator), the University of Edinburgh, Instituto de Telecomunicacoes, Naver Labs Europe and Unbabel. 


[EuroLLM](https://eurollm.io) is one of UTTER's most well-known outputs.
